Healing potion

A bitter potion that heals minor 
wounds, however, it does 
not restore fatigue.

1 clover + 2 green dropplet


Sleeping kiss

This potion produces deep
drowsiness. Especially useful
for those who suffer from
insomnia or need to recover
from fatigue.

3 Daisy + 2 phantom cloth


Love me potion

This potion connects the two
people who take it in a deep 
feeling of love. 
The effect is momentary. 
Overdoses are dangerous.

2 Creepy eyes + 2 red dropplets


Focusing water

This potion helps to enter a
state of hyperfocus, it can 
be dangerous if the person 
has any type of fatigue.

3 summer cloud + 3 blue dropplets


Confident push

This potion is especially useful
for those who are shy, or
particularly reserved, to show
what they truly feel or think.

3 orange dropplets + 2 poppy


Memory of sacrifice

This forbidden formula sacrifices 
life or memory in exchange for 
power. Even if used for the 
greater good, whoever uses it 
will pay a huge price.

3 purple dropplets + 3 forbidden meat + 3 mandrake


Sacred tears

They say an angel, moved by
the kindness of a human, offered 
its bottled tears to grant her 
a wish. It dispels any curse 
or black magic.

5 yellow dropplets + 5 bottled soul + 5 lucky clover
